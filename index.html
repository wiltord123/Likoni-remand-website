Welcome to Likoni Remand Home

The Likoni Remand Home provides care, rehabilitation, and education to young offenders and offers vocational training in carpentry, tailoring, and agriculture. Stay tuned for our upcoming community outreach event showcasing the talents and progress of our residents.

Located in the heart of Likoni, our remand home is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for young offenders. We focus on rehabilitation through structured programs and community involvement, aiming to reintegrate residents back into society as responsible individuals.
